Comparisons

Use your imagination, the pictures, and the word(s) to make your own comparisons.

Devised by Panagiotis Domvros, English and Drama Teacher.

1. Winter

Example: Winter in the mountains is more beautiful than winter in the city.

2. A Turtle - Rabbit

Example: The turtle is slower than the rabbit, but the rabbit is more arrogant.

3. Difficult

Example: This is the most difficult exam I have ever taken.

4. Your Christmas Tree

Example: Your Christmas tree is taller than ours this year.

5. Expensive

Example: This is the most expensive watch in the entire store.

6. Summer

Example: For me, summer is the best season for vacation.

7. Plane

Example: The new airplane is more spacious than the older model.

8. My House

Example: My house is the most comfortable place in the world.

9. Sally

Example: Sally is the most intelligent student in her class.

10. Melisa

Example: Melisa has a friendlier smile than anyone I know.

11. Reading a book

Example: Reading a book is more interesting than playing video games.

12. Tom's Exam

Example: Tom's exam result was better than he expected.

13. My Cat

Example: My cat is the fluffiest cat in the neighborhood.

14. Short

Example: This pencil is shorter than that one.
